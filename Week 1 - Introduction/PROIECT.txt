COACHR

You are a sports coach for a popular sports team (football, basketball, handball, etc.). You use a mobile device (tablet/phone) to access real-time data regarding performance, tactics, statistics, and more. During live matches, you also use a pair of special MR glasses to view live data mapped around the field and the players (including competitors). Moreover, with your special smartwatch, you can quickly communicate instructions to your staff.  

Design for: Tablet/Mobile Phone, AR Headset, Smartwatch
